My Experience at Christmas Fun Session Today

the exam ends, we have a fun session at our tution. Recently, our PT2s(Periodic
Tests) ended. Our techer told that we would celebrate the CID (Children's
Independence Day(From exams)) fun session at Christmas so it would be fun.
Mam told that everyone can bring a board game, so I took Chess there.
But, when we started mam said that we're not playing

out from the game in the 1st round. Mam also told that
in the Final(Annual) Exams, if everyone brings full marks
